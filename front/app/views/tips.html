Tips

Some tips to be ready when your product will be on Product Hunt.

Must be done before the launch

(meaning before launch day's morning)
  • Find a hunter who is going to get your product featured on the front page.
  • Come up with a tagline which describes your product in less than 60 characters.
  • Find the makers' twitter handles. If your product is featured by someone else, ask a PH moderator on Twitter to add your teammates to the conversation.
  • A few great pictures/screenshots of your product.
  • Let your friends, family and supporters know that you will be featured on PH.

During the storm

  • Try to be the first to comment on your product, give your product's story, share your vision and mention your team.
  • Never ask for upvotes!
  • Don't neglect any comment. Try to answer everyone's comments with a minimum of people trolls.
  • Stay alert on social networks (using Hootsuite, Buffer... or search Twitter for "myproductname producthunt"), answer questions, retweet (cleverly) and try to generate interaction around your product.
