About

What's going on here?

Product Hunt users can now search for a word and find matching hunters to contact in order to ask them hunt your project / app / startup.

Who made this?

The guy behind this is @Clemkn, a CS student, Product Hunter, maker of Bookstck and co-captain of the Nalyze ship.

All HunterHunt related code is available on Github.

How it works?

Thanks to Product Hunt's API, a set of scripts counts all occurrences contained in best hunters' taglines and associates a batch of words to each hunter.

For example, here is the list words associated with @rrhoover, CEO of Product Hunt:
game, photo, gifs, music, friend, video, ios, product, track, mobile and others.

How many hunters are available?

Currently, HunterHunt only analyses the 85 best hunters. All the hunters are available here.
